Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Applicable Application: John Clips Stuff

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to John Clips Stuff ("we," "our," or "us"), a desktop application that processes video files locally on your machine and publishes them to social media platforms via official APIs. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the John Clips Stuff application.

Data Processing

Local Processing: All video processing (clipping, reframing, transcription, captioning) happens entirely on your local machine. Your video files are never uploaded to our servers.

Social Media Publishing: When you choose to publish, processed clips are sent directly from your machine to the social media platform's official API (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram). We do not intermediate or store these uploads.

Information We Collect

Account Credentials: When you use John Clips Stuff, OAuth tokens for social media platforms are stored locally on your machine. These are used solely to authenticate with each platform's API for publishing content.

Usage Data: John Clips Stuff does not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics. The application operates entirely offline except when publishing content.

Data Sharing

John Clips Stuff does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Video content is only transmitted to the social media platform you explicitly authorize through John Clips Stuff.

Third-Party Services

John Clips Stuff integrates with:

  • TikTok (via Content Posting API)
  • YouTube (via YouTube Data API v3)
  • Instagram (via Instagram Graph API)

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle uploaded content.

Data Retention

John Clips Stuff does not retain any of your data on remote servers. Local project files (transcripts, clips, configuration) created by John Clips Stuff remain on your machine until you delete them.

Security

John Clips Stuff stores OAuth tokens locally on your machine. These tokens are not transmitted to any third party outside of the authorized OAuth flow with each social media platform.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the John Clips Stuff application: johnclipsstuff@gmail.com

Changes

John Clips Stuff may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
