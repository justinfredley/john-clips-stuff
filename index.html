John Clips Stuff

Desktop application that processes long-form videos into short vertical clips with captions and publishes them to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

Features

  • Batch processing of long-form video into short clips
  • Automatic 9:16 vertical reframing with blurred background
  • AI-powered speech transcription and captioning
  • Scheduled multi-platform publishing
  • Local processing - your content stays on your machine
